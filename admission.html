Admission Process

Step 1: Make an Enquiry and Book a Tour

Get in touch with our Admissions team to find out about learning opportunities at AIA, how to join our school community, and visit our campuses and facilities.

Step 2: Apply for the Admissions Assessment

The placement assessment will be paper-based, as well as the oral interview.

Step 3: Wait for the Results

Our admissions team will reach out to you within 24 to 48 hours after your child has completed the placement test.

Step 4: Accept Your Enrolment Offer and Submit the Required Documents

After passing the placement test, an enrolment offer will be made to you. Documents can include copies of student information.
