Welcome

Albert International Academy (AIA), where serious learning is fun. We pride ourselves on being a friendly learning school and giving a warm welcome to students and visitors. Our utmost aim at AIA is to ensure that all students are happy and successful in school.

Why choose AIA?

Western Australian Curriculum

Your child is able to study this world-class curriculum without leaving their own country. Our school is licensed and accredited by SCSA to teach the Western Australian curriculum, which is recognized and respected globally for its rigorous academic standards and emphasis on critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. This means that your child will receive an education that is not only relevant to the global marketplace but also prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century.
