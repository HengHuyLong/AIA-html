About Us

About us

Our Vision

Our vision is to be committed to the pursuit of academic excellence, enabling students and staff in realizing their potential within a safe, positive, and caring environment where all students and staff have the opportunity to work hard to achieve their personal best.

Our Mission

AIA strives to provide our students with a balanced and well-rounded education to develop them to their full potential while nurturing them into good citizens who are conscious of their responsibilities to family and society.

Our Objective

Our objective is to help our students discover their talents, realize their potential, and develop a passion for learning that lasts them through their lives.

Our Philosophy

The curriculum at AIA is shaped by our Mission and Vision statements, which are used to guide learning through a variety of experiences, including self-directed exploration, student-teacher collaboration, and peer-to-peer learning.

Why Choose AIA?

Western Australian Curriculum

Your child is able to study this world-class curriculum without leaving their own country. Our school is licensed and accredited by SCSA to teach the Western Australian curriculum, which is recognized and respected globally for its rigorous academic standards and emphasis on critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. This means that your child will receive an education that is not only relevant to the global marketplace but also prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century.

Global Recognition

The Western Australian curriculum is recognized and respected globally, and is considered to be one of the most rigorous and comprehensive curricula in the world. By offering this curriculum, Albert International Academy can provide your child with a globally recognized education that opens doors to top universities and career opportunities.

Exemption from IELTS and Possible Scholarships

Once your child graduates from our school and obtains their year 12 Western Australian Certificate of Education (WACE), they may be exempt from taking the IELTS test when applying to universities in other countries. Not only are they exempt from this test, but if they receive a high rank in year 12, they may even be eligible for an international scholarship. This exemption is typically based on the recognition of a high standard of English language education provided by schools such as ours.

Albert International Academy is bringing a world-class education to your child.
